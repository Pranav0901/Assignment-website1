Plot

Wealth, fame, power. Gold Roger, The King Of the Pirates, obtained this and everything the world has to offer.

"You want my treasure? You can have it. I left everything in that one place, now you just have to find it!" -His dying words drove countless men to the sea, in pursuit of the dreams greater than they even dared to imagine. This was the time known as 'The Great Pirate Era'

The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit. With his crew of pirates, named the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy explores the Grand Line in search of the world's ultimate treasure known as "One Piece" in order to become the next Pirate King. ->Read more
